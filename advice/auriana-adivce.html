Hello My Friend,
I have some advice for you.

Learn to think
like a developer

If you don't know any developers in person, listen to Podcasts. Find podcasts about developers and coding so you can listen to people who are doing it right now.

Learn from the way they speak the problems that they faced, and how they overcame them. Even if you do not understand everything- you will understand the value it has- and that is priceless!

Wake up 1 hour Early

It can be hard to add more to our already full and busy lives.

So if your day is already packed- then add an extra hour to the beginning of your day- and code. Make a project. Just do something regularly and you will grow so much!

Make Friends

When you need help or are stuck on a project- you should do the read/search method. This is an essential skill to develop. But if the answer or help you need is not out there-turn to your friends.

Everyone talks about community and it's important to join one, but what if you are shy or introverted and not the type of person to speak with strangers online?.

Do it anyway. If you get on the chats and slack regularly, people will begin to recognize your name.
